How it works

Personalised to you

  • Your pay, your way — Use your payscale or enter your exact rate from your payslip
  • Casual friendly — 8% holiday pay added automatically
  • Deductions sorted — KiwiSaver, Student Loan, NZNO Levy, secondary tax

More than a calculator

See the full picture

  • Real take-home — Know exactly what hits your account
